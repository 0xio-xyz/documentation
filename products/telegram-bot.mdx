# 0xio bot User Guide

Welcome to the official documentation for **0xio bot**, a Telegram bot designed to monitor your **Octra Network** addresses in real-time. Receive instant notifications for incoming and outgoing transactions, track your balances, and manage multiple wallets directly from Telegram.

## Getting Started

To start using the bot, open [@NullXio_bot](https://t.me/NullXio_bot) in Telegram and tap the **Start** button or send the command:

```bash
/start
```

You will be greeted by the main menu, which gives you quick access to your wallets, settings, and tools to add new addresses.

---

## Key Features

* **Real-Time Monitoring**: Get notified instantly when you send or receive OCT coins.
* **Multiple Wallets**: Track up to **5 different Octra addresses** simultaneously.
* **Privacy Control**: Toggle balance visibility in notifications to keep your holdings private.
* **Detailed Insights**: View transaction hashes, counterparty addresses, and direct links to the Octra Explorer.
* **Address Validation**: Built-in checks ensure you only track valid Octra addresses.

## Managing Wallets

### Adding a Wallet

1. Click **Add Wallet** in the main menu or send `/add`.
2. Paste your **Octra Network address**.

> **Note:** Addresses must start with `oct` and be 47 characters long.

3. Enter a **custom name** for the wallet (e.g., "Main", "Savings").
4. The bot will sync your balance and begin watching for new transactions immediately.

### Viewing & Editing

Access your wallet list by sending `/wallets`. Click on any wallet to:

* **Rename**: Change the display label.
* **Delete**: Stop tracking the address.
* **Explorer**: View the address on the official Octrascan explorer.

### Wallet Limits

Each user can watch a maximum of **5 wallets**. If you reach this limit, you must delete an existing wallet before adding a new one.

## Settings & Privacy

You can customize how notifications appear in your chat.

### Notification Balance

By default, the bot displays your updated wallet balance in every transaction notification.

* **Visible**: Shows `Balance: 150.0 OCT` in the alert.
* **Hidden**: Removes the balance line for privacy (useful if you share screenshots or use the bot in public).

**To toggle this setting:**

1. Send `/settings`.
2. Click the **Hide Balance** or **Show Balance** button.

## Notification Types

When a transaction occurs on the Octra Network, you will receive a notification similar to this:

**Received Transaction**

> **RECEIVED** 100.0 OCT
> From: [oct...abcd](https://octrascan.io/addresses/oct...abcd)
> Wallet: [Main](https://octrascan.io/addresses/your-wallet-address)
> Balance: 1250.0 OCT

**Sent Transaction**

> **SENT** 50.0 OCT
> To: [oct...xyz1](https://octrascan.io/addresses/oct...xyz1)
> Wallet: [Main](https://octrascan.io/addresses/your-wallet-address)
> Balance: 1200.0 OCT

*Clicking the links in the notification will take you directly to the transaction details on the blockchain explorer.*

## FAQ

**Q: Why does the bot say "Invalid Address"?**
A: Ensure your address starts with `oct` and contains exactly 47 alphanumeric characters.

**Q: Can I track the same address on multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, the bot tracks public blockchain data, so an address can be monitored by multiple users.

**Q: How fast are notifications?**
A: The bot polls the Octra Network approximately every 30 seconds to check for new activity.
