## Overview

**0xio DEX** is the premier **Automated Market Maker (AMM)** built for the **Octra Network**, leveraging **Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)** to enable truly private trading.

### The Problem with Traditional DEXs

On standard decentralized exchanges:

- Your trade sizes are publicly visible
- Front-running bots exploit your transactions
- Trading strategies become transparent
- Competitors can track your activity
- MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) extraction

### The 0xio DEX Solution

Trade tokens with **amount privacy** using FHE:

- **Input Privacy:** Swap amounts encrypted on-chain
- **Hidden Balances:** Account balances remain confidential
- **Front-Running Protection:** Bots can't see your trade size
- **Strategy Privacy:** Your trading patterns stay private

## Making a Private Swap

For confidential, front-running resistant trading:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Private Balance">
    Ensure sufficient **Private Balance**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable FHE">
    Toggle FHE shield to ON
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Tokens">
    Choose trading pair
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Input swap amount
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Encrypted Details">
    Review (amount will be encrypted):
    - Token pair
    - Estimated output range
    - Gas fee (higher for FHE)
    - Slippage settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute Swap">
    Click "Swap"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Local Encryption">
    Your device encrypts the swap amount
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Approve encrypted transaction
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Processing">
    5-10 seconds for encrypted transaction
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How Private Swaps Work

1. **Device Encryption:** Your browser performs FHE encryption on swap amount
2. **Encrypted Broadcast:** Encrypted payload sent to blockchain
3. **Private Validation:** Smart contracts validate on encrypted data
4. **Encrypted Execution:** AMM processes swap without seeing amounts
5. **Private Receipt:** Tokens delivered to your Private Balance
6. **Zero Leakage:** Nobody sees your trade size

### Private Swap Details

- **Speed:** 5-10 seconds
- **Privacy:** Amounts fully encrypted
- **Gas Cost:** ~40,000-50,000 OU
- **Best For:** Large trades, strategic positions

<Warning>
Private swaps require sufficient **Private Balance**. If your funds are in Public Balance, encrypt them first using the Privacy tab.
</Warning>

## Slippage Protection

Control how much price movement you'll tolerate:

### Slippage Settings

| Setting | Recommended For |
|---------|----------------|
| **0.1%** | Stablecoins, high-liquidity pairs |
| **0.5%** | Standard tokens, moderate liquidity |
| **1.0%** | Lower liquidity, volatile pairs |
| **Custom** | Advanced users, specific strategies |

### Setting Slippage

1. Click settings icon
2. Select or enter slippage percentage
3. Higher slippage = more likely to execute, worse price
4. Lower slippage = better price, might fail

<Info>
During high volatility, consider increasing slippage to ensure your swap executes. You'll never pay more than your slippage tolerance.
</Info>

## Transaction States

### Pending 

- Transaction submitted to DEX contract
- Awaiting validator confirmation
- Typically 3-10 seconds
- Can't be cancelled once submitted

### Confirmed 

- Swap executed successfully
- Tokens in your wallet
- Transaction hash available
- View in block explorer

### Failed 

- Transaction rejected or reverted
- Funds safely returned
- **Common causes:**
  - Insufficient balance for gas
  - Price moved beyond slippage tolerance
  - Insufficient liquidity
  - Network connectivity issue

## Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient Balance for Swap"

- Check you have enough tokens to cover swap amount + gas
- Verify correct balance type (Public vs Private)
- Ensure gas fee reserve (minimum 50,000 OU worth)

### "Slippage Tolerance Exceeded"

- Price moved beyond your tolerance
- Increase slippage tolerance
- Try again when market is less volatile
- Break large trades into smaller chunks

### "Swap Taking Too Long"

- Network congestion can delay processing
- Check transaction status in History tab
- Wait up to 60 seconds during peak times
- Increase gas fee for future trades

### "FHE Swap Failed"

- Ensure sufficient Private Balance
- Verify FHE operations are not rate-limited
- Check network status
- Try public swap as alternative

<Tip>
For urgent trades during congestion, use custom gas fees to set higher OU for priority processing.
</Tip>
